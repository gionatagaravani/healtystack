# Classic Thanksgiving Turkey Recipe

Learn how to prepare a perfectly roasted turkey that's juicy on the inside and golden brown on the outside - the centerpiece of your Thanksgiving feast.

## Ingredients

- 12-14 pound whole turkey, thawed
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 3 tablespoons fresh herbs (sage, thyme, rosemary)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon, zested and quartered
- 1 onion, quartered
- 2 carrots, roughly chopped
- 2 celery stalks, roughly chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 cups chicken broth

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325째F (165째C)
2. Remove giblets and pat turkey dry
3. Mix butter with herbs, garlic, and lemon zest
4. Gently loosen skin and rub butter mixture under it
5. Season cavity with salt and pepper
6. Stuff with lemon, onion, and some herbs
7. Place vegetables in roasting pan
8. Place turkey on top of vegetables
9. Pour broth in pan
10. Roast for 15-20 minutes per pound
11. Baste every 30 minutes
12. Rest for 20-30 minutes before carving

## Tips

- Bring turkey to room temperature before cooking
- Use a meat thermometer to ensure doneness (165째F/74째C)
- Tent with foil if browning too quickly
- Save pan drippings for gravy
- Calculate cooking time based on weight
