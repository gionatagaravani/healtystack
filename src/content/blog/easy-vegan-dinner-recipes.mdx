    Amount

    Garlic

### Instructions:

- Cook pasta according to package instructions

- Blend soaked cashews, plant milk, and nutritional yeast until smooth

## Frequently Asked Questions


### How can I ensure I'm getting enough protein in these vegan meals?

These recipes incorporate protein-rich ingredients like legumes, quinoa, and tofu. Additionally, you can boost protein content by adding seeds, nuts, or plant-based protein powder to your meals.



### Can I meal prep these vegan dinner recipes?

Most of these recipes are excellent for meal prep. You can prepare ingredients in advance, store them properly in the refrigerator, and assemble them when needed. Many of these dishes taste even better the next day as flavors continue to develop.
