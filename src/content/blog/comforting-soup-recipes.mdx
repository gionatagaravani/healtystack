## 1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup





    
        Ingredient
        Amount
    
    
        Chicken breast
        2 pounds
    
    
        Egg noodles
        2 cups
    
    
        Carrots, diced
        3 medium
    
    
        Celery stalks, diced
        3 stalks
    
    
        Onion, diced
        1 large
    
    
        Chicken broth
        8 cups
    
    
        Fresh thyme
        2 sprigs
    
    
        Bay leaves
        2
    




### Instructions:


    - In a large pot, bring chicken broth to a simmer.

    - Add whole chicken breasts and cook for 20 minutes.

    - Remove chicken, shred it, and set aside.

    - Add vegetables to the broth and simmer for 10 minutes.

    - Add noodles and cook until tender.

    - Return shredded chicken to the pot.

    - Season with salt and pepper to taste.
