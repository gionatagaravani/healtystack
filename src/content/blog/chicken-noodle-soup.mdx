# Chicken Noodle Soup

There's nothing quite as comforting as a warm bowl of homemade chicken noodle soup. This classic recipe combines tender chicken, hearty vegetables, and perfectly cooked noodles in a flavorful broth.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 carrots, sliced
- 3 celery stalks, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
- 2 cups egg noodles
- 1 teaspoon thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
2. Saut√© onions, carrots, and celery until softened
3. Add garlic and cook for another minute
4. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil
5. Add chicken and noodles
6. Cook until noodles are tender (about 6-8 minutes)
7. Season with thyme, salt, and pepper
8. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving

## Tips

- Use rotisserie chicken for a quick shortcut
- Make extra and freeze for later
- Add extra vegetables for more nutrients
- Cook noodles separately if planning to store leftovers

Enjoy this soul-warming soup any time you need a comforting meal!
