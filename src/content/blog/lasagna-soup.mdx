# Lasagna Soup

Experience the delicious flavors of lasagna in a warm, comforting soup form. This innovative recipe combines all your favorite lasagna ingredients in a hearty soup.

## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups broken lasagna noodles
- 24 oz marinara sauce
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 cup ricotta cheese
- 1 cup mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- Fresh basil for garnish
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in a large pot
2. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened
3. Pour in marinara sauce and broth
4. Add Italian seasoning
5. Bring to a boil and add broken lasagna noodles
6. Cook until noodles are tender (about 8-10 minutes)
7. In each bowl, add a dollop of ricotta
8. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan
9. Garnish with fresh basil

## Tips

- Break lasagna noodles into bite-sized pieces
- Make it vegetarian by using plant-based ground meat
- Add spinach for extra nutrition
- Prepare cheese mixture ahead of time

A creative twist on the classic Italian favorite!
