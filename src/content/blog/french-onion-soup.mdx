# French Onion Soup

This rich and flavorful French onion soup features caramelized onions in a savory broth, topped with crusty bread and melted Gruyère cheese.

## Ingredients

- 6 large onions, thinly sliced
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 cups beef or vegetable broth
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Crusty bread slices
- 2 cups grated Gruyère cheese

## Instructions

1. In a large pot, melt butter with olive oil over medium heat
2. Add sliced onions and sugar, cook until deeply caramelized (about 45 minutes)
3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
4. Pour in wine and scrape up any brown bits
5. Add broth, bay leaf, and thyme
6. Simmer for 30 minutes
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Ladle into oven-safe bowls
9. Top with bread and cheese
10. Broil until cheese is bubbly and golden

## Tips

- Take your time caramelizing the onions - it's worth it!
- Use good quality cheese for the best results
- Serve immediately while the cheese is hot and stretchy
- For vegetarian version, use vegetable broth instead of beef

A perfect soup for cold winter evenings or whenever you crave something truly comforting!
