# No-Bake Christmas Dessert: A Festive Treat Without the Heat


During the holiday season, the oven is often working overtime with all the baking demands. That's why having a delicious no-bake dessert recipe in your arsenal is a game-changer. This festive no-bake Christmas dessert combines the classic flavors of chocolate, peppermint, and cookies to create a stunning layered treat that will wow your guests. While traditional holiday baking like [Christmas sugar cookies](https://healtystack.com/easy-christmas-sugar-cookies/) is wonderful, this no-bake option saves time and oven space while still delivering that perfect holiday sweetness.



## Ingredients


    
        Ingredient
        Amount
    
    
        Chocolate sandwich cookies (like Oreos)
        24 cookies
    
    
        Unsalted butter, melted
        1/3 cup
    
    
        Cream cheese, softened
        16 oz
    
    
        Heavy whipping cream
        2 cups
    
    
        Powdered sugar
        1 cup
    
    
        Peppermint extract
        1 teaspoon
    
    
        White chocolate chips
        12 oz
    
    
        Candy canes, crushed
        4-5 pieces
    
    
        Green food coloring
        2-3 drops
    




## Step-by-Step Instructions


### Base Layer Preparation

1. Line a 9x13 inch pan with parchment paper, leaving some overhang for easy removal.


2. Crush the chocolate sandwich cookies in a food processor until they form fine crumbs.


3. Mix the cookie crumbs with melted butter until well combined.


4. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Place in refrigerator.



### Cream Cheese Layer

5. In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy.


6. Add 1/2 cup powdered sugar and beat until well combined.


7. In a separate bowl, whip 1 cup of heavy cream until stiff peaks form.


8. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture.


9. Spread this layer evenly over the cookie base.



### Peppermint Layer

10. Melt white chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring between each.


11. Once melted, add peppermint extract and green food coloring.


12. Whip the remaining heavy cream with the rest of the powdered sugar.


13. Fold the melted white chocolate mixture into the whipped cream.


14. Spread this layer over the cream cheese layer.



### Final Touches

15. Sprinkle crushed candy canes over the top.


16. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

## Frequently Asked Questions


### How long can I store this dessert?

This dessert can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. For best results, wait to add the crushed candy cane topping until just before serving to prevent it from becoming sticky.



### Can I make this dessert without peppermint?

Absolutely! You can omit the peppermint extract and candy canes, replacing them with vanilla extract and different toppings like chocolate shavings or seasonal sprinkles for a different but equally delicious variation.
