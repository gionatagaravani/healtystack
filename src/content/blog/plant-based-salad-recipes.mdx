## 1. Mediterranean Quinoa Power Bowl



    
        Ingredient
        Amount
    
    
        Cooked quinoa
        2 cups
    
    
        Cherry tomatoes
        1 cup, halved
    
    
        Cucumber
        1 medium, diced
    
    
        Kalamata olives
        1/2 cup
    
    
        Red onion
        1/4 cup, diced
    
    
        Fresh parsley
        1/4 cup, chopped
    
    
        Lemon juice
        2 tablespoons
    
    
        Extra virgin olive oil
        2 tablespoons
    




### Instructions:


    - Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool

    - In a large bowl, combine cooled quinoa with tomatoes, cucumber, and olives

    - Add diced red onion and chopped parsley

    - Whisk together lemon juice and olive oil

    - Pour dressing over salad and toss gently

    - Season with salt and pepper to taste

## FAQ


### How long do plant-based salads stay fresh in the refrigerator?

Most plant-based salads will stay fresh for 3-5 days when stored properly in an airtight container in the refrigerator. However, it's best to store dressings separately and add them just before serving to maintain optimal freshness.



### Can I meal prep these salads?

Absolutely! Many of these salads are perfect for meal prep. Layer ingredients with heartier vegetables at the bottom and leafy greens at the top. Store dressings separately and add just before eating. Most will last 3-4 days when properly stored.



### What can I use to replace oil in salad dressings?

There are several oil-free alternatives for salad dressings, including pureed avocado, hummus, mustard, or tahini thinned with water or lemon juice. These options provide creaminess while keeping the dressing wholesome and nutritious.
