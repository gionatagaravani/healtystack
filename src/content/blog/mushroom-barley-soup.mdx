# Mushroom Barley Soup

This rustic mushroom barley soup combines earthy mushrooms with nutty barley for a satisfying and wholesome meal.

## Ingredients

- 1 pound mixed mushrooms, sliced
- 1 cup pearl barley
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 cups vegetable broth
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon rosemary
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
2. Saut√© mushrooms until golden brown
3. Add onion, carrots, and celery, cook until softened
4. Add garlic and herbs, cook for 1 minute
5. Add barley and broth
6. Simmer for 45 minutes or until barley is tender
7. Add soy sauce
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Garnish with fresh parsley

## Tips

- Use a mix of mushrooms for more complex flavor
- Rinse barley before cooking
- Add more broth if soup becomes too thick
- Can be made ahead and reheated

A perfect comfort soup for mushroom lovers!
