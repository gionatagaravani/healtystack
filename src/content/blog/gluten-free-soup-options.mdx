## Classic Vegetable Quinoa Soup (Gluten-Free)


### Ingredients:



    Ingredient
    Amount


    Quinoa (rinsed)
    1 cup


    Carrots (diced)
    2 medium


    Celery stalks (chopped)
    3 stalks


    Onion (diced)
    1 large


    Garlic (minced)
    4 cloves


    Gluten-free vegetable broth
    8 cups


    Diced tomatoes
    1 can (14 oz)


    Italian seasoning
    1 tablespoon


    Fresh spinach
    2 cups


    Olive oil
    2 tablespoons


    Salt
    To taste


    Black pepper
    To taste





### Instructions:


1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add diced onions and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until translucent.



2. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.



3. Add diced carrots and celery. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.



4. Pour in the gluten-free vegetable broth and diced tomatoes. Bring to a boil.



5. Add rinsed quinoa and Italian seasoning. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until quinoa is cooked.



6. Add fresh spinach and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until wilted.



7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.



### Serving Suggestions:


Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired. This soup pairs wonderfully with [gluten-free quesadillas](https://healtystack.com/gluten-free-cheeseburger-quesadillas/) for a complete meal. You can also top it with [various gluten-free toppings](https://healtystack.com/savory-casserole-toppings/) like nutritional yeast or dairy-free cheese.

## Frequently Asked Questions


### Q: Can I freeze this gluten-free soup?

A: Yes! This soup freezes beautifully. Store it in an airtight container for up to 3 months. When ready to eat, thaw overnight in the refrigerator and reheat gently on the stovetop.



### Q: How can I ensure my soup is truly gluten-free?

A: Always check labels carefully, especially on broths and seasonings. Look for certified gluten-free products, and be aware that some processed ingredients may contain hidden gluten. When in doubt, make your own broth from scratch using verified gluten-free ingredients.
