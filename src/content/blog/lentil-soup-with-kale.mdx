# Lentil Soup with Kale

This nutritious lentil soup combines protein-rich lentils with fresh kale and aromatic vegetables for a healthy and satisfying meal.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups green or brown lentils
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 carrots, chopped
- 3 celery stalks, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bunch kale, stems removed and chopped
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 8 cups vegetable broth
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Lemon wedges for serving

## Instructions

1. Rinse and sort lentils
2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
3. Saut√© onion, carrots, and celery until softened
4. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
5. Add lentils, broth, and seasonings
6. Simmer for 20-25 minutes until lentils are tender
7. Add chopped kale and cook for 5 more minutes
8. Season with salt and pepper
9. Serve with fresh lemon wedges

## Tips

- Use pre-washed, chopped kale for convenience
- Add more vegetables like sweet potatoes or butternut squash
- Make it spicier with red pepper flakes
- Store leftovers in the fridge for up to 5 days

A perfect healthy meal that's both filling and nutritious!
